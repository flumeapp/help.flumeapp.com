# Reset Flume

If you are looking to remove Flume of all your account information, simply use the [logout functionality](/getstarted/login.md). This will remove any traces of your account information from Flume's local database and storage.

If you wish to completely reset Flume, including resetting all [Preferences](/preferences/README.md), [Flume Pro](/preferences/flumepro.md) status, caches and account information, hold down the <kbd>‚å• (OPTION)</kbd> key as soon as you launch Flume. You will be asked to confirm that you wish to reset Flume.
